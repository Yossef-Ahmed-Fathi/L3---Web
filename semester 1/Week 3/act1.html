Online Services

In this activity, you will explore various online services and their impact on our daily lives. You will learn about the benefits and challenges of using online services, as well as how to use them safely and responsibly.

Website Development

Web development encompasses designing and building websites through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It combines frontend design with backend server logic, database management, and security protocols. Modern web development requires knowledge of responsive design, user experience principles, and various frameworks to create dynamic, interactive applications serving millions of users worldwide today.

Video Editing

Video editing involves assembling and refining footage to create compelling visual content. It encompasses cutting, transitions, color correction, and audio synchronization. Professional editors use specialized software like Adobe Premiere and DaVinci Resolve. With social media's growth, video editing has become essential for content creators and filmmakers producing engaging multimedia worldwide.

Desktop Apps

Desktop applications are software programs installed directly on computers, offering powerful functionality without internet dependency. They provide superior performance, offline access, and immersive user experiences. From productivity suites to gaming engines, desktop apps deliver advanced features and customization. While requiring installation, they remain essential for professional work, creative projects, and demanding computational tasks.
