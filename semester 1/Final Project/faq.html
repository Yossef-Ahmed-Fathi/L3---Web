Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is LaunchPad Live and what does it offer?
LaunchPad Live is an online programming academy dedicated to teaching web development. We provide structured, hands-on tracks designed to take you from an absolute beginner to a job-ready front-end developer.
2. What technologies will I learn in the web development track?
Our comprehensive foundational track covers the core essentials of front-end development:
  • HTML5: To build the structure of web pages.
  • CSS3: To style and design beautiful layouts.
  • Bootstrap: To create fast, modern, and fully responsive websites.
  • JavaScript: To add logic, interactivity, and dynamic features to your projects.
3. Why are the courses taught entirely in English?
Since English is the universal language of technology and global business, all our content is delivered in English. This ensures you become familiar with standard industry terminology and are fully prepared to pass international technical interviews.
4. Do I need any previous coding experience to enroll?
No prior experience is required! Our curriculum is designed from scratch specifically for absolute beginners. We guide you step-by-step through the basics before moving on to advanced concepts.
5. Is the learning style theoretical or practical?
We strongly believe in learning by doing. Our courses are highly practical and project-based. Throughout the track, you will build real-world websites and applications to establish a strong personal portfolio.
6. Are the classes live or pre-recorded?
Our courses consist of high-quality, pre-recorded video lessons. This self-paced format allows you to study whenever and wherever it fits your schedule, without worrying about rigid timetables.
7. Will I get help if I get stuck on a coding bug?
Yes, absolutely! We provide a dedicated community and support section where you can ask questions, share your code, and get assistance from instructors and fellow students whenever you face a problem.
8. How long will I have access to the course materials?
Once you enroll in a course, you get lifetime access to all its videos, updates, and learning materials. You can revisit the lessons whenever you need a refresher.
9. Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes! Once you successfully complete the course requirements and finish the practical projects, you will receive a digital certificate from LaunchPad Live to showcase on your LinkedIn profile or resume.
10. How can I get started?
Getting started is simple! Just create a free account on our platform, browse the available courses, choose your track, and start your coding journey instantly.
